Our Story

Trusted Auto Repair Since 1995

AutoFix Masters was founded by John Masters, a passionate mechanic with over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry. What started as a small garage with just two service bays has grown into a full-service auto repair facility with state-of-the-art equipment and a team of certified mechanics.

Our mission is simple: to provide honest, reliable, and high-quality auto repair services at fair prices. We believe in transparency, integrity, and building long-term relationships with our customers based on trust and exceptional service.

Today, AutoFix Masters is proud to be a family-owned business that continues to serve the community with the same dedication and commitment to excellence that has defined us from the beginning.

Our History

1995

AutoFix Masters was founded by John Masters in a small two-bay garage.

2002

Expanded to a larger facility with five service bays and added diagnostic equipment.

2008

Received ASE Blue Seal of Excellence recognition for our commitment to quality.

2015

Celebrated 20 years of service and renovated our facility with state-of-the-art equipment.

2020

Expanded services to include electric and hybrid vehicle repairs and maintenance.

Today

Continuing to grow while maintaining our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction.
